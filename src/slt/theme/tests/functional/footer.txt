Log in as Manager::

    >>> browser.login(TEST_USER_NAME, TEST_USER_PASSWORD)
    >>> print browser.bycss('#portal-footer')
    --- 1 ---
    <div id="portal-footer">
    </div>

Add footer::

    >>> browser.open('/createObject?type_name=Document')
    >>> browser.getControl(name="title").value = 'Footer'
    >>> browser.getControl(name="text").value = '<p>Text of Footer.</p>'
    >>> browser.getControl(name="form.button.save").click()
    >>> print browser.bycss('#portal-footer')
    --- 1 ---
    <div id="portal-footer">
    <p>Text of Footer.</p>
    </div>
